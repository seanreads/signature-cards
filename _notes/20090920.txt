Scenario A

Author creates card
Card is locked - no more editing
Author sends to signers a "signers URL' known only to signers
Signers sign card
Author sends link to recipient w/"published URL" known only to author
Once link sent to recipient, signatures are locked

Author signs card
Author sends to signers a "signers URL' known only to signers
Author sends card
